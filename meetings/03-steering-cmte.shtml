3rd Meeting of the Steering Committee

On 8 November 2015, the third meeting of the International Asteroid Warning Network (IAWN) Steering Committee was held at National Harbor, Maryland (near Washington, DC). The agenda is available here.

The presentations prepared for this meeting are available below:

  • ESA SSA Status
    Detlef Koschny (ESTEC)
  • IAWN Status
    Rob Landis (NASA Headquarters)
  • Real-time Characterization of Hazardous Asteroids: Lessons from Recent Flybys
    Vishnu Reddy (PSI)
  • NEOWISE Update; NEOCam in Phase A
    Amy Mainzer (JPL)
  • Communication Planning, One Year Later: Time for Action
    Linda Billings (National Inst. of Aerospace/NASA Headquarters)
  • Words matter: A call for responsible communication about asteroid impact hazards and plans for planetary defense
    Linda Billings (National Inst. of Aerospace/NASA Headquarters)
  • Building International Infrastructure for Planetary Defense
    Linda Billings (National Inst. of Aerospace/NASA Headquarters)
    Sergio Camacho (UNCOPUOS)
