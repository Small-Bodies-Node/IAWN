2nd Meeting of the Steering Committee

On November 11, 2014, the second meeting of the International Asteroid Warning Network (IAWN) Steering Committee took place in Tucson, AZ, coinciding with the week-long American Astronomical Society's Division of Planetary Sciences meeting.

The presentations prepared for this meeting:

  • IAWN: Recent Updates and MPC Summary
    Tim Spahr (MPC)
  • IAWN Communication Workshop notes
    J.L. Galache (MPC)
  • Asteroid Lightcurve Database
    Brian Warner (Center for Solar System Studies)
  • Rapid-Response Characterization of Near-Earth Objects
    N. Moskovitz (Lowell Observatory)
  • NEOWISE/WISE Characterization Results
    J. Bauer (JPL)
  • Arecibo Radar Observations of Near-Earth Asteroids
    P.A. Taylor (Arecibo Observatory/USRA)
