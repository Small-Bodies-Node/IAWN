What's going on with Comet 2I/Borisov?

Borisov's Lightcurve


Lightcurve of comet 2I/Borisov (colored dots), compiled from observations reported to the Minor Planet Center1 (solid circles) and the Comet Observer's Database2 (open circles).


Status Updates

2020 Apr 2: 2I/Borisov appears to have fragmented (ATEL 13611).

2019 Aug 30: Discovered by Gennady Borisov at the MARGO observatory.
